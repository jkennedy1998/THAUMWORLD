# diet
> the resources someone needs to sustain life

DIET represents the resources a CHARACTER must consume to remain functional.

Every CHARACTER must satisfy their DIET within 10 EXTENDED ACTIONS.
> roughly one day

If a CHARACTER has any of the TAGS listed below as part of their DIET,
they are able to consume those resources.

A CHARACTER must consume a total SIZE MAG equivalent to their DIET requirement.
> usually SIZE MAG 2 for player CHARACTERS, or two edible ITEMS

If a CHARACTER does not consume their required DIET within an EXTENDED ACTION,
they take 1 VIGOR DAMAGE.

> this page represents the DIETs / creature types for the LAND OF SNOW AND FOG setting.
> other planets may have different creature types like silica based life.
---

## diet tags

### herba
Carbon based producer life.
> plants, mushrooms, decomposers  

---

### chlorba
Chlorine based producer life.
> geotroids, slime gems  

---

### carna
Carbon based consumer life.
> birds, apes, dragons  

---

### chlarna
Chlorine based consumer life.
> billows, blimps, gem heads, gooplings  

---

### fabra
Thaumic based collector life.
> crystallizers, sprites  

---

### casta
Thaumic based manipulator life.
> post-dead, golems, diffusers, sprites, mages  

---

### materia
Raw non-organic material.
> iron, stone, gold, quartz
