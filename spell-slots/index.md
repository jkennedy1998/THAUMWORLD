# spell-slots
> how many spells a character knows at one time
SPELL SLOTS represent how many SPELLS a CHARACTER has memorized and ready.

A SPELL SLOT represents memorization and attunement to a SPELL.

A SPELL SLOT can be edited by taking an EXTENDED WORK ACTION.
ROLLS are usually not required.

CALLS dictate the maximum number of GLYPHS that can be placed into a SPELL SLOT.
