# armors
> items that that empower characters through tags

ARMORS are just ITEMS that have a META TAG of ARMOR and can be EQUIPT in BODY SLOTs.
ARMOR applies only to attacks that hit the BODY SLOT of the ITEM
> helmets don’t help your leg

ARMOR has REQUIREMENTS like TOOL, and has required BODY SLOTS that the ARMOR can be EQUIPT on.
> armor requiring brawn mag 1 AND a covered wing body slot for m- characters
If you do not meet the REQUIREMENTS of the ARMOR, the ARMOR cannot be EQUIPT on a BODY SLOT
> you may still be able to hold it as an ITEM in a HAND, but thats not the point and procs no armor bennefits

ARMOR has a BREAK THRESHOLD set by the ARMOR TAG
> see BREAK THRESHOLD for more info

ARMOR WEIGHT contributes to WEIGHT PENALTY when EQUIPTED.
