# thaum-n-pools
> magical energy, life force, or electricity 2

THAUM is the energy spent to CAST SPELLS.

THAUM can be regenerated by SLEEP or REPAIR EXTENDED ACTIONS
when VIGOR is spent to HEAL.

---

## thaumapacity
> how much thaum can be moved

THAUMAPACITY is how much THAUM a CASTER can move per CAST.

> like electrical capacity, but for magic

---

## pools
> where thaum exists

A POOL refers to collected THAUM within a connected system of touching ITEMS or CHARACTERS.

> catalysts, tools, and the mage holding it
