# dissonance-n-harmony
> a budget to add positive and negative modifiers to the content of a STORY BEAT

## DISSONANCE 
measured in MAG, how many negative modifiers can be in a STORY BEAT, given by the STORY FLOW of a STORY

## HARMONY 
measured in MAG, how many positive modifiers can be in a STORY BEAT, given by the STORY FLOW of a STORY
