# dissonance-n-harmony
> a budget to add positive and negative modifiers to the content of a STORY BEAT 

## DISSONANCE 
measured in MAG, given by the STORY FLOW of a STORY
can be spent to make things harder with the DISONANCE MAG SHOP on the CONTENT TYPES TABLEs

## HARMONY 
measured in MAG, given by the STORY FLOW of a STORY
can be spent to make things easier with the HARMONY MAG SHOP on the CONTENT TYPES TABLEs
