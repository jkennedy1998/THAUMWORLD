# dice-rolls-n-nat
> defining uncenrtainty

the DICE used are for ROLLS are :
- D2 
- D4 
- D6 
- D8 
- D10
- D12 
- D20

ROLLS happen to determine uncertain outcomes.
>trying to hit something, calculating damage, generating loot

the word ROLL will be used synonymously with the outcome of the ROLL.
>" What is your RESULT ROLL? ", " Gimme your POTENCY ROLL? "

NAT refers to a natural landing dice face.
>a NAT20 is a D20 that has landed on 20, regardless of EFFECTORS

there are two ROLL types in this game:
RESULT ROLLs and POTENCY ROLLs. identity.
