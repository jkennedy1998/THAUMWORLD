# archetype-table
> a list of scalable contents for story-beats and content types

## ARCHETYPE TABLE 

### enemy-protected-loot
LOOT is seen protected by an ENEMY who can trigger a TIME BASED EVENT if the ACTORS get too close or mess with the LOOT without pacifying the ENEMY
> a wild beast protects its large delicious eggs in its nest

> a wild beast which can be harvested unwillingly has something loot worthy like hardened hide, special horns, or alchemical pieces of their body like organs.

> a small shop in a city is protected by a guard. the coolest thing in the shop is the loot. you can buy this to pacify the guard, or take it in a way unwilling to the guard

> a piece of loot is found in a quiet environment. its a trap, an enemy is using it as bait

### the-zelda-boss
LOOT is protected behind an +1 MAG enemy, with a PUZZLE allows for additive MAG against the enemy
> a giant plant monster with big bulbs of flowers with massive staymens  and spore eminating from them. 

> these specific BODY SLOTs of the ENEMY are weak to something

> the mplant monster has a sword (loot) stuck in the side of the body

> INFORMATION is present of how the sword got there.

### betrayal
a CHARACTER goes from ALLY to ENEMY in order to get an ITEM or INFORMATION from you
> they may try to hide things from you.

> they may try to steal things from you

> they may sell a piece of info out for coin or favor

## stray
tracks of a CHARACTER are found straying from the quest path. at the end of the tracks is an ITEM under a PUZZLE

> a catch / storage place hidden from others like a small animals den or a hiding place

> a few corpses on the side of the road with some small loot items on them
