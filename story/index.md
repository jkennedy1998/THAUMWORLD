# story
> has beats that happen, and a flow that controls those beats

a STORY is an abstraction of how a story is told, with different pieces within. 
the STORY is not the end of the game, but it does grant one PERK POINT to all ACTORS when it is completed.

a STORY has : 

## STORY BEATS 
> a list of STORY BEATs

## CURRENT STORY BEAT : 
> the place in the list of STORY BEATs

## STORY FLOW : 
how the story beats are made.
on creation of a STORY choose a random STORY FLOW from the STORY FLOW TYPES
