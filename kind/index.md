# kind
> what species someone is that determines other aspects chosen in CHARACTER CREATION

KIND defines a CHARACTER’s baseline body, senses, movement, and cultural context.
it is chosen during CHARACTER CREATION and provides defaults for multiple tracked values.

## KIND perk rules
on CHARACTER CREATION, each CHARACTER picks 2 GIFT OF KIND perks.
each CHARACTER also picks 1 GIFT OF GREATER KIND perk.
all FLAW OF KIND perks listed for their KIND apply automatically.

- RIGHT ARM
- LEFT LEG
- RIGHTLEG

### HEALTH

EXTENDED ACTION: SLEEP

### AGE
- adulthood : 20
- decline : 40
- death : 50

### LANGUAGE
- APISH, vocal

### SIZE
- M+

### SENSES
- LIGHT : capable
- AROMA : impaired
- PRESSURE : capable
- THAUMIC : blind

### SPEED
- walk : 6
- climb : 1
- swim : 2
- fly : none

### STAT changes
- DEX + 2 (+1 mod)

### DIET
- herba
- carna

### TEMPERATURE RANGE
- -1 MAG to +1 MAG

### APPEARANCE
bipedal creatures that resemble earth’s humans in most ways. they grow hair from their head, and some from their bodies.

### LORE
the naked apes are the smallest civilization of apes by number. their cultures revolve around trade, communication, and craft.

the NAKED APES have settled in the north east region of THE LAND OF FOG AND SNOW, where coastal winds keep them in some sort of warmth.  there they have made good allies with the reptiles of the CLEAR COASTS.

### GIFT OF KIND
CRAFTY KIND : GIFT OF KIND
you PEAK 1 less for the CRAFT ACTION
( this helps you make +1 items more often )

THE ENDURING APES : GIFT OF KIND
you only need 3 SLEEP EXTENDED ACTIONS per 10 EXTENDED ACTIONS,
( compared to other sleep reliant kinds who require 4 )

AMONG THE APES : GIFT OF KIND
your CLIMBING SPEED raises to 2

FABULOUS FILANGIES : GIFT OF KIND
gain +1 REROLL when using the CRAFT sub ACTION
( this helps you make +1 items more often )

### FLAW OF KIND
LONG WAY FROM HOME : FLAW OF KIND
you are gain -1 REROLL for
RESULT and POTENCY rolls, favored against you
( getting rid of disease, not getting disease, taking damage from )
