# potency-rolls
> how to tell how potent or powerful something is

POTENCY ROLLs measure the potency of an ACTION.

> how hard you stabbed that guy
> how loud you slammed that door
> how hot you got the fire

POTENCY ROLLs have a MAGNITUDE.
this tells you what to ROLL.

> you can find an easy reference chart in the MAGNITUDE section

POTENCY ROLLs have EFFECTORS.
these encompass ARMOR, PERK bonuses, and WEAKNESSES.

> a weakness in a structure
> armor blocking incoming damage
> monster weak points being hit

POTENCY ROLL DICE:
> correlates with MAG

POTENCY ROLL effectors:
> + 1 PROFICIENCY MAGNITUDE (not the roll, just a single bonus if its really relevant.)
> + infinite EFFECTORS
