# body-slots-n-equipt
> how you equip items, tools, and armor

ITEMS can be EQUIPPED on a CHARACTER through BODY SLOTS.
BODY SLOTS represent parts of a CHARACTER.
BODY SLOTS can be cut off, replaced, modified, or can be a TARGET.
Some BODY SLOTS are vital and lead to immidiate DEATH if you lose them
> heads, torsos, some bug butts. 
> each CHARACTER has their own vital BODY SLOTS.

HAND SLOTS are BODY SLOTS.
Not all BODY SLOTS are HAND SLOTS.

each BODY SLOT can equip 1 ITEM with a tag that allows for it.
> Some ARMOR may specify exceptions to this allowing for multiple pieces of armor.
> like rings!

each HAND SLOT can equip 1 ITEM regardless of tags.
> but some tags make it better suited as a TOOL

An ITEM in a BODY SLOT is EQUIPPED.
An ITEM in a HAND SLOT is EQUIPPED as a TOOL.

A CHARACTER with nothing in their HAND SLOTS
uses their hands as TOOLS.

> hands and improvised objects count as MAG 1 TOOLS by default
> a MAG 1 roll is a 1d2, or a coin

all EQUIPMENT applies its weight towards the WEIGHT PENALTY at 1 per 5 lbs
>tools and armor on your character
