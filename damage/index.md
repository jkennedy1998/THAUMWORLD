# damage
> harmful changes to health, vigor, or thaum

DAMAGE is subtracted from the HEALTH, THAUM, or VIGOR of a CHARACTER.
If not stated otherwise, DAMAGE applies to HEALTH.

DAMAGE does not have types.
DAMAGE may have one or more TAGS.

DAMAGE can be represented using MAG by referencing the MAG CHARTS
in the DAMAGE, EFFECT, and PAIN sections.
