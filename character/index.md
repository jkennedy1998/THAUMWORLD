# character
> anything that can do actions

a CHARACTER can do things through ACTIONS
these are usually living things and traps
> an animal that can attack, a player character who can use, a trap that has a hold

## what makes up a CHARACTER
a CHARACTER has the following. each item is represented on their CHARACTER SHEET

> HEALTH a measurement of life in a physical and short termed sense with a maximum and current

> VIGOR a measurement of your well being in a long term sense with a maximum, current and VIGOR MAG
> ACTIONS : how many things you can do in a TIMED EVENT on one TURN
> whether you SLEEP or REPAIR as an EXTENDED ACTION to regain HEALTH
> how much SLEEP or REPAIR you need per day ( 10 extended actions )
> PARTIAL ACTIONS : how many things you can do in a TIMED EVENT as a bonus, and very quickly
> KIND, what species someone is that determines other aspects
> STATISTICS, six general measurements CONSTITUTION, STRENGTH, DEXTERITY, WISDOM, INTELEGENCE, CHARISMA 
> PROFFICIENCIES, a count of how MAGNITUDE you have of the 21 different PROFFICICENCIES
> SEX from their KIND's availible SEXes ( usually male or female )
> SIZE measureed in MAGNITUDE from their KIND's SIZE
> AGE measured by year within the ranges from KIND
> MOVEMENT a measurement of four tracked speeds walking, climbing, swimming, and flying, from KIND
> SENSES, a measurement of four senses for light, aroma, pressure, and thaum, from KIND
> TEMPERATURE RANGE : a lower MAGNITUDE for cold, and a higher MAGNITUDE for hot from KIND
> EVASION, how well you can dodge physical attacks
> FORTITUDE, how well you can dodge mental and magically tracking attacks
> CALLS, how many glyphs you can cast during a SPELL
> SPELL SLOTS, how many spells you know at once
> THAUM, a measurement of magical energy with a maximum and current 
> THAUM REGEN, how much thaum you regen per SLEEP or REPAIR
> CARRY CAPACITY, how much weight you can carry before getting debuffed
> EQUIPTMENT WEIGHT, how much your current EQUIPT ITEMS weigh
> INVENTORY, a list of ITEMS which have names, weight, and tags
> INVENTORY WEIGHT, the weight of all your INVENTORY
> WEIGHT PENALTY, a measurement of your weight against your ability to dodge getting hit
> BODY SLOTS, where ARMOR can be EQUIPT
> HAND SLOTS, like body slots but you can also EQUIPT TOOLS
> TAGS, a list of what TAGs are currently on your character
> LORE, a txt of where that character came from, what they are doing, what their goals are, ect
> PERKs, upgrades bought through the PERK TREE with PERK POINTS given on LEVELING UP
> PERK POINTS, points awarded on LEVELING UP
> LEVEL, an abstract of how far a CHARACTER has come since the story started
