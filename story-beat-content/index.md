# story-beat-content
> what happens during a story beat, set by the archetype table and modulated with MAG

## CONTENT TYPES TABLE

### ENEMY-CHARACTER
a CHARACTER which has a GOAL may oppose yours, but doesnt neccesarily have to do with you particularly
the CHARACTER should be equal or within 1d4 MAG of the ACTOR's CHARACTER MAG ( general power level and amount of perks)
they are hostile and will attempt to harm you if you get in the way of their GOAL
if the ACTORS help the CHARACTER complete their goal, they may become less hostile, or even allies.
> negative mag boosts them by one perk
> 0 mag spawns the CHARACTER as is.
> positive mag weakens them by one flaw 

### ALLY-CHARACTER
a CHARACTER which has a GOAL of their own that has nothing to do with yours.
the CHARACTER should be equal or within 1d4 MAG of the ACTOR's CHARACTER MAG ( general power level and amount of perks)
they are un-hostile and will assist you if you assist them, but they will not travel off the path of their GOAL unless they have to.
if the ACTORS help them complete their GOAL, they will have another goal after resting or doing mundane tasks for 1d4 days. 
these CHARACTERs could become large moving pieces of the STORY if choose to follow nearby ( they have to get something equal out of it )
this CHARACTER is simply spawned or loaded in as an ALLY, they may not be an ALLY forever.
> negative mag weakens them by one flaw 
> 0 mag spawns the CHARACTER as is.
> positive mag boosts them by one perk

EQUIPTMENT : equipment is targeted, or found
> negative mag means ACTORs ITEMs will be TARGETED by an ENEMY in theft if not BATTLE is present, or by attacking it if there is a BATTLE present
> 0 uses the positive, but the LOOT is MAG 0. ( mundane clothes, garb, or material that can be made into clothes )
> positive mag means the ACTORS find LOOT that can be EQUIPT by a HAND

INFORMATION : some information granted to the players 
> negative mag gives some lore that isn't helpful to move towards the STORY END
> 0 is just mundane descriptor
> positive mag gives diegetic motivation moving towards the STORY END

## ARCHETYPE TABLE 

### enemy-protected-loot
LOOT is seen protected by an ENEMY who can trigger a TIME BASED EVENT if the ACTORS get too close or mess with the LOOT without pacifying the ENEMY
> a wild beast protects its large delicious eggs in its nest

> a wild beast which can be harvested unwillingly has something loot worthy like hardened hide, special horns, or alchemical pieces of their body like organs.

> a small shop in a city is protected by a guard. the coolest thing in the shop is the loot. you can buy this to pacify the guard, or take it in a way unwilling to the guard

> a piece of loot is found in a quiet environment. its a trap, an enemy is using it as bait

### the-zelda-boss
LOOT is protected behind an +1 MAG enemy, with a PUZZLE allows for additive MAG against the enemy
> a giant plant monster with big bulbs of flowers with massive staymens  and spore eminating from them. 

> these specific BODY SLOTs of the ENEMY are weak to something

> the mplant monster has a sword (loot) stuck in the side of the body

> INFORMATION is present of how the sword got there.

### betrayal
a CHARACTER goes from ALLY to ENEMY in order to get an ITEM or INFORMATION from you
> they may try to hide things from you.

> they may try to steal things from you

> they may sell a piece of info out for coin or favor
